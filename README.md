## 🎓 Foundation — Anthropic AI Fluency for Students

Before this advanced journey, I completed the **Anthropic AI Fluency for Students** certification. That course gave me the **4D Framework** — four competencies for responsible, effective AI use:

| D | Name | What It Means |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | **Delegation** | Deciding what YOU do vs what AI does |
| 2nd | **Description** | Clearly communicating what you need |
| 3rd | **Discernment** | Evaluating AI's output critically |
| 4th | **Diligence** | Taking responsibility for everything AI helps create |

This foundation is what makes the advanced learning meaningful — I'm not just learning techniques, I'm building on a framework for **responsible, fluent AI use.**
